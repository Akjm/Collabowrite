Collabowrite

Play with 3 or more people for maximum chaos.

This text will be replaced with whatever you write below. After that, the text will again be replaced by the new sentence. Pass the keyboard around to your friends and watch the story evolve. Once you are finished typing press pass the keyboard, and to view the whole story press end story. Remember to use proper grammar with a period at the end and a capital at the start.

Once you're done adding to the story, pass the keyboard to the next person or press end to view the entire story and finish the session.
